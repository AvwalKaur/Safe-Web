🐾 Step-by-Step Guide to Use SafeWeb Extension 🐾

🚀 Step 1: Install the Extension
Download 📥 the SafeWeb Toxicity Filter from the Chrome Web Store.

⚙ Step 2: Set Your Preferences
Open the extension pop-up.
Choose the Sensitivity Level (Low, Medium, High)
from the drop down.

🔎 Step 3: Enable blur (optional)
Toggle "Enable Blur" to hide harmful messages.

💾 Step 4: Save Your Settings
Click "Save Preferences" ✔ to apply your chosen settings.

📡 Step 5: Real-Time Toxicity Detection
The extension automatically scans messages and detects toxicity.
Harmful messages are flagged, blurred, or hidden based on your settings.

📩 Step 6: Receive Alerts via Gmail
If a message crosses the set Toxicity Threshold, an alert is sent via Gmail for immediate action.

🌏 Step 7: Get to Know More
Click "Get to Know More" for additional details.
Visit the official website to stay updated.
